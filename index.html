What do we provide?

Alef Ba Ta teaches Arabic language to children from age 4 - 10 years using joyful and fun interactive lessons.
Children will enjoy learning Arabic without any needed previous knowledge in the language.


Games


Hundreds of educational games that introduces
Arabic alphabets, simple words and
construction of short sentences.


Sight Words


Introduce top 100 sight words in an engaging
way and help the child to easily memorize them.

Exercises

Thousands of interactive exercises that cover
basics of Arabic starting with alphabets and
pronunciations up to pronouns and conjugation.

Stories

Collection of stories arranged in five levels.
Every story comes with exercises that focus on
language construction & comprehension skills.


Texts


Collection of Arabic texts for older kids, with an
objective of enriching the Arabic language and
introducing social values.


Grammer


Interactive exercises and printables that focus
on teaching Arabic grammar for elementary
stage.

Alef Ba Ta for Schools

Alef Ba Ta provide schools with interactive
educational content to make learning Arabic more
engaging and fun. Teachers will have access to a
wide range of learning materials that can help
capture the attention of their students and get them
more exicted to learn.

Accounts for teachers & students Control educational content Create and assign homework Monitor students performance
